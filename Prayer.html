Dear God,
Bless Taiwo, a ray of love in his house and a protector of safety in his workplace. Give him guidance and insight as he faces the difficulties of his work life and looks out for the welfare of people entrusted to his care. Give him the fortitude to assume leadership roles at work and fill his house with the warmth of your heavenly love. Good health, happiness, and harmony for his family, especially for Ayanfe. May Taiwo's life serve as an example of your grace, bringing safety and kindness to the office and love inside his family's walls.
I ask in Your Name.
Amen.
